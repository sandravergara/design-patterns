STEP 6: Press the "Start to Compile"
STEP 7: After you compile, go to the Run tab section beside the Compile Tab. Change the account address to the last
		this would be, "0xca35b7d915458ef540ade6068dfe2f44e8fa733c"

STEP 9. Enter the corresponding inputs.
STEP 10. Paste the contents into get hash button and click the arrow to expand it again and paste the corresponding inputs
STEP 11. Click get HASH.
